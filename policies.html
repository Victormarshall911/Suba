SUBA Refund Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

SUBA is committed to providing fast, secure, and reliable digital utility services. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be granted.

Eligible Refund Requests

Users may qualify for a refund if:

  • Payment is successful, but the purchased service is not delivered within the stated timeframe.
  • A transaction fails due to a verified technical issue on SUBA's platform.
  • Duplicate charges occur for the same transaction.
  • A user is incorrectly debited due to a system error.

Non-Refundable Transactions

Refunds will not be provided if:

  • Incorrect phone numbers, meter numbers, smart card numbers, or account details are submitted by the user.
  • The service provider confirms successful delivery.
  • The user changes their mind after a successful purchase.
  • Service disruptions occur after successful delivery due to third-party network providers.
  • Fraudulent or suspicious activity is detected.

Refund Process

  1. Submit a refund request within 72 hours of the transaction.
  2. Provide the transaction ID and any relevant information or screenshots.
  3. SUBA will investigate the claim within 1–3 business days.
  4. Approved refunds will be processed within 5–10 business days.

SUBA reserves the right to request additional information before approving any refund.

SUBA Know Your Customer (KYC) Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

SUBA uses a risk-based KYC process to verify user identities and maintain platform security.

Tier 1 – Basic Verification

Requirements:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • OTP verification

Features:

  • Access to basic services
  • Lower transaction limits

Tier 2 – Standard Verification

Additional Requirements:

  • National Identification Number (NIN) or Bank Verification Number (BVN)

Tier 3 – Enhanced Verification

Features:

  • Access to premium features
